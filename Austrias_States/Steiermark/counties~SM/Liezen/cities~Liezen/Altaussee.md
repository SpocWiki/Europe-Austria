## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Altaussee** (German: [ˈalt.aʊ̯sˌseː]; Central Bavarian: Oid Aussee) 
> is a municipality and spa town in the district of Liezen in Styria, Austria. 
> 
> The small village is nestled on the shores of the Lake Altaussee, beneath the Loser Plateau. 
> 
> Occupying an area of 92 km2, Altaussee is home to 1,777 people. 
> 
> The municipality includes two cadastral communities: Altaussee and Lupitsch. 
> 
> The designated climatic spa is within the Salzkammergut region. 
> Altaussee has the biggest salt deposits of Austria, which are still mined today.
>
