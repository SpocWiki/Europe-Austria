## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The University of Vienna (German: Universität Wien, Austrian German: [univɛrsiˈtɛːt ˈviːn]) 
> is a public research university in Vienna, Austria. 
> 
> Founded by Duke Rudolph IV in 1365, 
> it is the oldest university in the German-speaking world 
> and among the largest institutions of higher learning in Europe.
>
> The University of Vienna is associated with 17 Nobel Prize laureates 
> and has been home to numerous scholars of historical and academic significance, 
> including Erwin Schrödinger, Karl Popper, Stefan Zweig, Friedrich Hayek, Gustav Mahler, Sigmund Freud, Gregor Mendel, Ludwig von Mises, among others.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University%20of%20Vienna)
