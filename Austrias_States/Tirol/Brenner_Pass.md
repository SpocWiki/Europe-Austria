# [[Brenner_Pass]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Brenner_Pass,212946]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Brenner Pass** (German: Brennerpass [ˈbʁɛnɐpas], in short Brenner; Italian: Passo del Brennero [ˈpasso del ˈbrɛnnero]) 
> is a mountain pass over the Alps which forms the border between Italy and Austria. 
> It is one of the major passes of the Eastern Alpine range 
> and has the lowest altitude among Alpine passes of the area.
> 
> The central section of the Brenner Pass covers a four-lane highway 
> and a railway corridor connecting Bozen/Bolzano to the south and Innsbruck to the north. 
> 
> The village of Brenner consists of an outlet shopping mall (supermarkets and stores), 
> fruit stores, restaurants, cafés, hotels and a gas station. 
> 
> It has a population of 400 to 600 (as of 2011).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brenner%20Pass)
