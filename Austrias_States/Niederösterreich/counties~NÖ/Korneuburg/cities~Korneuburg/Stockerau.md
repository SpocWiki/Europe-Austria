## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Stockerau** (German pronunciation: [ʃtɔkəˈʁaʊ] ) 
> is a town in the district of Korneuburg in Lower Austria. 
> 
> Stockerau has 16,974 inhabitants, which makes it the largest town in the Weinviertel. 
> 
> Stockerau is also called "Lenaustadt" (Lenau Town) 
> because the Austrian poet Nikolaus Lenau often spent time with his grandparents here.
>
